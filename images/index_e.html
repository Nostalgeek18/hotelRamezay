Located in Marieville, in the heart of Montérégie, is the Manoir Ramezay, a gem of a hotel offering exceptional accommodations. Located 20 minutes south of Montreal, between St Jean Sur Richelieu and Granby. Manoir Ramezay, also called Hotel Chambly, is an intimate inn or bed and breakfast (B&B) offering continental breakfast as well as high speed Internet access. Conveniently located less then 12 minutes from: Chambly, Richelieu, Rougemont, St Césaire, Mont St Grégoire, St Damase, St Jean Baptiste De Rouville, St Mathias and Ste Angèle De Monoir. The Manoir Ramezay is a popular boutique hotel offering 12 guest rooms and suites. In order to complete your stay, The Manoir's Hotel is proud to announce its brand new Health Center: Spa Ramezay. Surrounded by a magnificent atmosphere, with a touch of the Orient, Spa Ramezay is inviting you to live an experience of its very own, connected with your senses! A multitude of services are available: Massotherapy, Aesthetic, Balneotherapy, Body Treatments as well as Weight Loss Program & Treatments! With historic charm of long ago and contemporary comfort of today, please let the Manoir Ramezay staff successfully arrange your events or stay with us so they are filled with unforgettable moments.
