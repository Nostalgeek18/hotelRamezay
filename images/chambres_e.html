Cancellation policy

Cancellation with more than 7 days of the reservation, no charge will be billed.
Cancellation between 7 days and 48 hours, there will be no reimbursement but you can change the date.
Cancellation within less than 48 hours, the complete bill will be charged.
The total payment of the bill will be asked at your arrival.
Quebec hospitality tax of 3.5% per day per room not included.
All rates are in Canadian dollars and do not include federal and provincial taxes.
All rates are subject to change without prior notice.
