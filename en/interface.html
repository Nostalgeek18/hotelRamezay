Ramezay Manor, a charming, intimate hotel with a historical flavour

Consecrated on January 1, 1801, Ramezay Manor
was the first presbytery and chapel in the region.

The current owners have been lovingly restoring
this historic site for more than twelve years, turning Ramezay Manor into an intimate haven that’s
the perfect place to relax and replenish.

Whether for a peaceful retreat of relaxation in our health Spä, a romantic getaway or for business, the gracious decor and ambiance of Ramezay Manor are sure to make your special occasion a memorable event.
